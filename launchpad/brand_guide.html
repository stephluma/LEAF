Who we are

Foundational Elements

The mission, vision, and values that define the LEAF platform and guide every decision we make.

Mission

Empower employees to build digital solutions that enhance services to Veterans.

Vision

A VA where every process works seamlessly — driven by people, powered by LEAF.

Core Values

  • Human-centered service
  • Transparency & accountability
  • Collaboration over complexity
  • Respect for time, people, and process

Standards & References

Design Systems

LEAF is informed by these established government design systems. We don't implement every component, but they serve as the foundation for our standards around accessibility, visual language, and user experience.

public

U.S. Web Design System

The federal government's design system, maintained by GSA. USWDS shapes our approach to typography, color contrast, accessibility, and core UI patterns.

open_in_newVisit USWDS
style

VA Design System

VA's own design system built on top of USWDS, tailored for Veteran-facing products. It informs how we handle content structure, form patterns, and VA-specific component conventions.
