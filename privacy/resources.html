    LEAF Secure (LEAF-S)

    What is LEAF-S?

    LEAF-S is a secure variant of the LEAF platform designed for workflows that contain protected health information (PHI) or personally identifiable information (PII). It exists to protect sensitive data with tighter controls and visibility.

    Once certified, your site receives LEAF-S controls and will be monitored for continued compliance with sensitive data handling standards.

    What sites need LEAF-S?

    LEAF-S is required for sites that collect, display, or route PHI or PII.

    Some examples may include:

    • Full name combined with date of birth
    • Social Security Number (full or partial, depending on the process)
    • Medical or health information
    • Case details tied to an identifiable Veteran
    • Attachments containing identifiable information
    • Employee records
    • and more

    This list is not exhasutive. When in doubt, consult your local Privacy Officer.

    How to get started

    Once you have identified a sensitive field in your form:

    1. Path 1: Use the link in the banner that appears in the Form Editor to begin the LEAF-S certification form or
    2. Path 2: Admin Panel > Site Settings > Start Certification Process.

    The LEAF-S certification process will automatically route the request to your local Privacy Officer. They will need to review and approve before a site is LEAF-S certified.

    Need more detail or support? Visit the LEAF-S Help Library for step-by-step guidance.
