Platform Updates for Enterprise Social Security Number Reduction (ESSNR)

Light Electronic Action Framework (LEAF) Platform is supporting the Enterprise Social Security Number Reduction (ESSNR) initiative by limiting the collection and use of full Social Security Numbers (SSNs) in LEAF-managed forms and workflows.

Summary

  • Full SSN collection is restricted and must be justified and approved.
  • Last 4 SSN is not included in this scope.
  • Unapproved full SSN collection will be escalated and may result in field removal.

Local LEAF Process Owner Responsibilities

When a site needs to use a full SSN
  1. The Process Owner provides a written justification for why a full SSN is required.
  2. The ESSNR team reviews the justification and either approves or denies it.
  3. The LEAF Information Security Officer (ISO) will facilitate communications between the Process Owner and ESSNR.
If a site uses a full SSN without approved justification

If a LEAF form includes a field collecting a full SSN and no approved justification is provided, LEAF will take the following actions:

  • Coordination attempt: The LEAF team will contact the Process Owner to request justification.
  • Enforcement: If justification is not approved, the SSN field will be removed from the form to prevent further collection of full SSNs.
